Chef Banzai

Trained under Japanese sushi masters, since the age of 5 and for 72 years, Chef Banzai Sarumaku knows well how to fillet a fish by now. True to his family's Samurai heritage his filleting method is with sword, often accompanied by his notorious shreiking of famous samurai battle cries.
